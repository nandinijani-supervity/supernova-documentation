## Core Platform Capabilities

Supervity is built around **agentic systems** that can reason, plan, and act across tools and modalities.

Key capabilities include:

- **Agentic Workflow**  
  Multi-agent orchestration for complex, end-to-end operational processes.

- **Agentic RAG**  
  Source-grounded question answering over enterprise knowledge bases and documents.

- **Agentic OCR**  
  Intelligent document understanding, including complex layouts and semi-structured data.

- **Agentic Chat**  
  Secure, configurable conversational interfaces for internal and external use cases.

- **Agentic Voice**  
  Voice-driven interaction and automation across workflows and systems.

- **Co-browsing AI Agents**  
  Real-time guidance and assistance embedded directly inside applications.

These capabilities can be used independently or composed into **larger agent-driven operational systems**.

Supervity is designed from the ground up for **enterprise governance and control**.

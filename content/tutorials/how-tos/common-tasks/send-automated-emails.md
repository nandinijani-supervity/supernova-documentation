# How to Send Automated Emails

A quick guide to setting up automated email sending in your workflows.

## Quick Start

### Step 1: Start a New Workflow

Open the workflow creation interface and start a new conversation.

### Step 2: Describe Your Email Goal

Tell the agent what you want to send:

**Examples:**
- "Send a welcome email to new users"
- "Send daily report emails"
- "Send notification emails when tasks complete"

### Step 3: Provide Email Details

The agent will ask for:
- **Recipient**: Who should receive the email
- **Subject**: Email subject line
- **Content**: What the email should say

### Step 4: Review and Approve

Review the workflow plan and approve execution.

### Step 5: Execute and Save

Execute the workflow and save it for reuse.

## Common Patterns

### Pattern 1: Simple Email

**Request:**
> "Send an email to john@example.com with subject 'Welcome' and body 'Welcome to our platform!'"

### Pattern 2: Personalized Email

**Request:**
> "Send a personalized welcome email. Use recipient_name and recipient_email as inputs."

### Pattern 3: Bulk Emails

**Request:**
> "Send emails to a list of recipients. Each email should be personalized with the recipient's name."

### Pattern 4: Scheduled Emails

**Request:**
> "Send a daily summary email every morning at 9 AM"

## Email Content Tips

### Subject Lines
- Be clear and descriptive
- Include important information
- Keep it concise

### Email Body
- Use clear formatting
- Include all necessary information
- Add call-to-action if needed

### Personalization
- Use recipient names
- Include relevant details
- Customize based on context

## Troubleshooting

### Email Not Sending
- Check email service connection
- Verify recipient addresses
- Check for rate limits

### Wrong Recipient
- Verify email addresses in inputs
- Check workflow inputs
- Test with your own email first

### Formatting Issues
- Use plain text or HTML appropriately
- Test email rendering
- Check special characters
